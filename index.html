Who We Are

ObtainCXN is looking to become the first Rhode Island based technology company that specializes in IoT application development.

Our Mission

To provide world class customer service and technical expertise at an affordable price. Our mission is to ensure that business of all sizes can reap the benefits associated with the rapid rise of the Internet of Things (IoT).

Our Founder

David Landi is an IT professional with over 13 years of experience in the technology field with a diverse background consisting of experience gained through positions in software development, leadership, and sales. David also has degree's in Software Engineering and an MSIT from New England Institute of Technology
